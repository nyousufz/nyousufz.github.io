Clickmox – Mechatronics Engineering Intern

        I worked at Clickmox Solutions as a Mechatronics Engineering Intern from January 2017 to May 2017. I was directly involved in developing their newest laser based 3D scanning system. I along with my co-worker designed the first 3D scanning system that can be mounted on any type of vehicle. The scanner can be mounted to a drone, tripod, cart and any type of vehicle. The product is available right now in the market. I worked on designing and developing a scanning system that would allow the user to see a live map as the scanner scans. The system is under production right now and will be in the market very soon.
